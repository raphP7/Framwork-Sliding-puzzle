## Compilation
 make produce 6 exemples of games made with the framework:
 
  2048

  2048Plugin ( 2048 plus unlimited personalised pieces like : Destroy piece , *2 piece , /2 piece , negative pieces ... )

  SokobanFile

  SokobanManual

  TakinCHAR

  TakinINT



## Execution

 ./mainTakinCHAR

 ./mainSokobanFile plateau_Niveau1.xsb
